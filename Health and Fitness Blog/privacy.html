Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Health & Fitness Blog (“we”, “us”, or “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: name, email address when signing up or submitting contact forms.
  • Health-related data: any fitness metrics, workout history, or dietary logs you choose to share via comments or forms.
  • Usage & technical data: IP address, browser type, referring pages, operating system, device information, and analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to contact inquiries.
  • To personalize your experience, recommend content based on your interests.
  • To analyze site usage patterns via Google Analytics or similar tools.
  • To comply with legal requirements or protect our rights.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to recognize returning visitors, track preferences, and improve site performance. You can manage cookie settings via your browser, and third‑party tracking tools may provide opt‑out options.

  • We only share data with third-party service providers (e.g. email hosting, analytics) under contractual obligations to protect your data.
  • We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal data.
  • Withdrawing consent at any time.
